Energizing Fried Greens

This quick, healthy dish packs flavor and vitamins, giving you the energy you need to do stuff.

What You Will Need

  • Bok choy, kale, cabbage, swiss chard, endive... just your favorite thicc greens, washed, dried, and roughly chopped
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic, sliced
  • Fancy salt. Big flakes. You know what I mean. Even better if it's like... truffle flavored.
  • Optional tablespoon of lemon juice and zest of one lemon.

Directions

  1. Heat the oil over medium high heat.
  2. Dump in that garlic and toss that around for thirty seconds.
  3. Add your greens. Coat them in the oil and garlic mix and toss until lightly charred.
  4. Remove from heat and season with Big Salt.
  5. Optionally, toss in lemon zest and juice for that extra zing.
